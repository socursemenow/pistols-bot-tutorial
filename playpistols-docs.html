⚔️ Classic Mode

Sealed simultaneous duel — pick your cards, pray, and reveal.

How It Works

Type /duel @opponent classic. Your opponent clicks Accept. Both of you get a secret menu (only you can see it) to pick 4 cards. Once both players confirm, the bot reveals everything and resolves the duel step-by-step in the channel.

Blades (Melee Tiebreaker)

If pistols don't settle it, blades come out. It's rock-paper-scissors:

🔫 Pocket Pistol beats ⚔️ Behead beats 🤼 Grapple beats 🔫 Pocket Pistol
⚠️ Seppuku is also an option — you attempt a dramatic self-sacrifice. 20% chance you merely wound yourself. Otherwise... you die by your own hand. High risk, maximum drama.
🧠 Strategy Tips

Low fire pace is risky but can dodge high-pace opponents. High fire pace almost always hits, but you're vulnerable to early dodges.

Pick your dodge to counter what you think your opponent will choose. It's a mind game — that's the whole point.

Blades are only relevant if pistols don't finish it, so don't overthink them unless you expect a close duel.

🔫 PvP Mode

Turn-based tactical duel — play your hand, outthink your rival.

How It Works

Type /duel @opponent pvp. Once accepted, you take alternating turns using Discord buttons on a public embed. Each turn, play one card from your hand — either shoot or use a tactics ability. Use /pvp hand to check your remaining cards at any time.

Your Hand

🔫

Pace 6 — balanced
Pace 9 — high accuracy

Hit% = 50 + (pace−1) × 5

Each can only be used once

🔥

🏁

Both decks empty? Most HP wins

🧠 Strategy Tips

Don't fire all your shots early. Pace 9 has the best accuracy — save it for when buffs or Last Stand can amplify it.

Insult before their big shot. -10% on a Pace 3 card can be devastating.

Spare Round + Vengeful is a nasty combo: reclaim your best fire card, then boost its damage.

Gambit is high-risk. Best played when you're behind and have nothing to lose.

🏜️ Gauntlet (PVE)

Solo roguelike — one life, infinite floors, escalating danger.

Starting a Run

Type /adventure start. You begin at Floor 1 with base stats, no gear, and a handful of HP. Each floor is either a combat encounter (65–75% chance) or a hazard event (25–35%). Death is permanent — your run ends and everything resets.

Use /adventure status any time to check your current stats, gear, and floor.

Combat Actions

Before combat, you see three options: ⚔️ Fight, 🏪 Shop, or 🏃 Flee. Once in a fight, these are your moves:

🔫

Shoot

Hit% = 55 + Aim × 5
Crit% = 10 + Nerve × 5

🎯

Steady Aim

Skip this turn.
Next shot can't miss.

🗣️

Taunt

Skip this turn.
Enemy gets -20% hit next turn.

⚠️ The enemy always shoots back after every action you take — there are no free turns!

Your Stats

You start with base stats and level up by getting kills. Every 3 kills you pick 1 of 3 random stat upgrades.

🎯Aim+5% hit per point — your core damage stat

🧠 Gauntlet Strategy Tips

Early floors (1–3): Drunkards are weak. Save your coins and don't buy from the shop unless something great shows up.

Prioritize Grit early. +1 max HP per point keeps you alive through bad RNG. Cunning is secretly amazing — free damage soak on every hazard.

Steady Aim is your best friend against Bounty Hunters and Outlaws. Skip one turn for a guaranteed hit next turn.

Flee is risky. If you fail, you take 1 damage AND still have to fight. Only flee if your HP is critical and the enemy looks deadly.

Don't skip loot. Gear bonuses stack and compound with your base stats. A Legendary Weapon (+3 Aim = +15% hit) is game-changing.

🏠 Add to Your Server

Bring Pistols at Dawn to your own community.

Who is this for? Discord server admins or moderators who want to install the bot in their community so members can duel each other directly.

How to Invite

Get the Invite Link

Ask for the official invite link in the Pistols at Dawn Discord, or check pinned messages in the bot channel.

Authorize the Bot

Click the link, select your server from the dropdown, and click Authorize. You need Manage Server permission.

Done!

Slash commands register automatically. Your members can start dueling right away with /duel and /adventure.

Bot Permissions

When you authorize the bot, it will request these permissions. All are required for the bot to function properly:

PermissionWhy It's Needed
Send MessagesPost duel results, combat turns, and announcements
Embed LinksRich embeds for duel cards, leaderboards, and gear displays
Use External EmojisDisplay custom card and stat icons
Read Message HistoryEdit existing duel embeds during turn-based modes
Use Slash CommandsRegister and respond to all /commands

Optional: Duel Notification Channel

The bot can automatically post when on-chain duels are resolved on pistols.gg. To enable this, ask a Pistols admin to configure a notification channel for your server. The bot will then post live results there every time a duel finishes on-chain.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the bot cost anything?

No — the bot is completely free to add and use. All game modes (Classic, PvP, Gauntlet) work out of the box.

Do my members need a Starknet wallet to play?

No. The prototype duels (Classic, PvP, Gauntlet) are fully off-chain and run directly in Discord. No wallet, no gas fees, no tokens needed. Wallets are only needed for on-chain features like /balance and /login.

Can players from different servers duel each other?

Duels happen within the server where the command is used. Both players need to be in the same server. However, player profiles and wallet links carry across servers since they're tied to your Discord ID.

Can I restrict the bot to specific channels?

Yes — use Discord's built-in channel permissions. Go to the channel settings → Permissions → deny the bot's role access in channels you want to keep clean, and allow it only in your designated game or bot channels.

What if the bot goes offline?

Active duels and Gauntlet runs are saved automatically. When the bot comes back online, players can pick up where they left off — no progress is lost.

How do I get help or report a bug?

Head to the official Pistols at Dawn Discord server and post in the support or bug-report channel. The dev team is active and responsive.
